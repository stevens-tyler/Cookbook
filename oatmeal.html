Mediterranean Oatmeal

This recipe can serve for a delicious breakfast, lunch, or even dinner! It is a savory take on oatmeal and is bound to get your taste buds jumping.

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Sundried tomatoes
  • Vegetable broth mix
  • Greek yogurt
  • Goat cheese
  • Basil
  • Pine nuts (optional)

Directions

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a pot
  2. Add half a table spoon of vegetable broth
  3. Add handfull of Sundried tomatoes
  4. Add 1 cup of oatmeal and cook until preferred texture
  5. Mix in 1 table spoon of greek yogurt
  6. Mix 1/2 table spoon of goat cheese
  7. Garish top with fresh basil
Ingredient Amount
Oats 1 cup
Sundried tomatoes 1/4 cup
Vegetable broth 1 table spoon
Greek yogurt 1 table spoon
Goat cheese 1 cup
Basil 1 leaf
Pine nuts 1/4 cup
